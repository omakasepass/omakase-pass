The Counter for Two: private omakase for date nights and proposals.

An intimate omakase for two, prepared course by course at your own counter. The full experience, with the chef's whole attention on your evening.

$400 flat · a private omakase for two

Date nights · anniversaries · proposals · birthdays for two

There is a particular kind of quiet that settles over a counter set for two. No dining room to share, no reservation clock, no table turning behind you. Just a chef working the day's best fish in front of the two of you, one considered piece at a time. It is the whole point of omakase, made small and made private.

It's why The Counter for Two is our most requested way to mark the evenings that deserve it: a first date that wants to become a story, an anniversary, a proposal planned down to the last piece of nigiri. Your chef reads the pace of the night and lets it unfold: ichi-go ichi-e, one time, one meeting, an evening made once for the two of you.

Everything but the table is handled. Your chef brings the fish, the ingredients, and their own tools, and takes care of preparation, service, and cleanup. You provide the kitchen and the company.
