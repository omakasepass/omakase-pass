The Omakase Pass · Coming to Atlanta

Private omakase and a sushi chef at home, coming to Atlanta.

The counter is coming to Atlanta. A sushi chef arrives at your home and works in front of you, serving the best of the day's catch course by course. Omakase the way it was meant to unfold: unhurried, personal, and made for your table alone.

In Japan, omakase rests on a simple covenant: the guest entrusts the meal to the chef, and the chef answers with the best of that day: that fish, that season, that moment. The tradition has a name for it: ichi-go ichi-e, one time, one meeting. A meal that will never happen the same way twice. It's that moment we bring to your table.

We're assembling a small, vetted collective of Atlanta's most talented independent sushi chefs, the same way we did in Nashville, matching one chef to your occasion, in your own home. An intimate omakase for two for a date night or proposal, a seated chef-curated tasting for an anniversary or rehearsal dinner, or a live sushi station working the room for a bachelorette weekend or birthday. No restaurant reservation, no traveling celebrity chef's calendar. Just a craftsman, your kitchen, and an evening made once, for you.
