The Offer

Your craft, our customers.

The Omakase Pass is a booking platform, not a restaurant and not a staffing agency. We connect Nashville's best independent sushi chefs with people looking for private omakase and sushi experiences, and we stay out of your way while you cook.

  • Keep your name. Your brand, your menu, and your pricing stay yours. We never put our label over your work.
  • Customers already looking for you. We bring you guests actively searching for private omakase and sushi events. No marketing effort on your part.
  • We handle the logistics. Booking, deposits, scheduling, and guest communication are ours to manage. You focus on the food.
  • Simple, founder-friendly terms. A founding-chef commission of 12%, charged only when an event books. No exclusivity. No upfront cost.
  • Ground floor. We're selecting a small initial roster of Nashville chefs now, early enough that your input helps shape how the platform works.

Questions, answered.

How do you protect my brand and reputation?

Your name stays front and center. We present you as an independent chef, not an anonymous vendor, and we only match you to events that suit your style. Every booking is vetted on our side before it reaches you.

Do I have to be exclusive to The Omakase Pass?

No. There's no exclusivity of any kind. Keep your restaurant, your private clients, and any other work. We're simply another channel for the bookings you already want.

How and when do I get paid?

You're paid for every event you cook, with deposits collected up front so you're never chasing a guest for payment. We'll confirm exact payout timing and method with you during onboarding.

Who handles cancellations or difficult guests?

We do. Cancellations, rescheduling, and guest communication all run through us, backed by deposits taken at the time of booking. You cook; we handle the awkward parts.

Can I set my own availability?

Always. You tell us when you want to work, and we only bring you events that fit your calendar. Take as few or as many bookings as you like.
