Ichi-go ichi-e — One time, one meeting

In Japan, omakase is built on a simple covenant: the guest entrusts the meal to the chef, and the chef answers with the best of that day — that fish, that season, that moment. The tradition carries a name for it: ichi-go ichi-e. One time, one meeting. A meal that will never happen the same way twice.

The Omakase Pass brings that moment to your table. We work with a small, vetted collective of Nashville's most talented independent sushi chefs and match one to your occasion. No restaurant reservation six weeks out. No traveling celebrity chef's calendar. Just a craftsman, your kitchen, and an evening made once — for you.
