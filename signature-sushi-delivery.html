When the counter can't come to you, it arrives at your door. Each box is a chef's selection of the day, packed with the same care it would be plated with, then closed with a seal that carries your initials in the tradition of the hanko stamp. The opening is the moment: a box that already looks considered before a single piece is tasted.

It travels as a gift as easily as a night in. Send one to mark a birthday, a new home, or a quiet congratulations, and it arrives personal, sealed for the one it is meant for.

Every box is made by one of our vetted Nashville chefs and delivered ready to eat. You choose the occasion; we handle the sourcing, the craft, and the finish.
